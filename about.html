About

A2ACT is a non-profit organization teaching kids about technology, computer science, and the future of STEM. A2ACT stands for "Anak to Anak in Computing Technologies". "Anak" means "child" or "kid" in the Indonesian language. Teaching from one child to another, the term "Anak to Anak" falls into place.

Lessons are posted on Youtube and are spoken in both English and Bahasa Indonesia. Along with the video lessons, A2ACT also provides written materials for both students and teachers, which can be found in the description of each video or on our website in the "Materials" tab.
